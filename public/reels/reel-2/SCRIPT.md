# Reel 2: Speed Demo
## "I created a week's content in 5 minutes"

**Duration:** 30-45 seconds
**Platform:** TikTok, YouTube Shorts, Instagram Reels
**Style:** Time-lapse feel, impressive transformation

---

## HOOK (0-3 seconds)
**On-Screen Text:** "POV: You just created 7 days of content"
**Visual:** Timer showing 5:00 minutes
**Audio:** Trending sound with anticipation build

---

## SETUP (3-8 seconds)
**On-Screen Text:**
- "Monday morning"
- "Content calendar: EMPTY"

**Visual:**
- Show empty calendar view (use: `reel2-01-calendar.png` or blank calendar graphic)
- Stressed face emoji or expression

**Voiceover/Text:** "Zero posts scheduled. Deadline approaching."

---

## THE CHALLENGE (8-12 seconds)
**On-Screen Text:** "Challenge: 7 posts in under 5 minutes"

**Visual:**
- Start timer graphic
- Type first prompt (use: `reel2-02-prompt.png`)

**Text Overlay:** "Let's see if Socialway AI can do it..."

---

## RECORDING NOTES

If recording yourself:
1. Start with overwhelmed look at empty calendar
2. Show confidence when starting the challenge
3. Speed up clips during creation montage
4. Celebrate at the reveal

Editing tips:
- Use speed ramping (slow-mo to fast)
- Add timer overlay throughout
- Use satisfying "complete" sounds
- Quick cuts build excitement

Music suggestions:
- Trending "speed run" sounds
- Electronic with build-up
- Something with a drop at the reveal
