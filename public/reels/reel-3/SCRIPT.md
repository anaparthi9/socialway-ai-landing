## RECORDING NOTES

If recording yourself:
1. "Before" side: Look disappointed, frustrated
2. Transition: Light up with realization
3. "After" side: Confident, impressed smile

Editing tips:
- Use split-screen effect throughout
- "Before" side: Desaturated/dull colors
- "After" side: Bright, vibrant colors
- Sync transitions to music beat

Music suggestions:
- Trending "glow up" sounds
- Transformation audio (record scratch to beat drop)
- Before/after reveal sounds

## ALTERNATIVE FORMATS

**Version A: Side-by-side split screen**
- Both visible throughout
- Highlight switches between sides

**Version B: Full-screen comparison**
- Show "before" full screen (5s)
- Transition
- Show "after" full screen (5s)
- End with comparison

**Version C: Swipe reveal**
- "Before" post full screen
- User "swipes" to reveal "after"
- Popular on Instagram
