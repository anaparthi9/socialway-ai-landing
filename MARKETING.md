## Decision Framework

### When to Increase Budget
- Cost/Conversion < $30 for 7+ days
- Conversion rate > 3%
- Consistent daily conversions
- **Action**: Increase budget by 20-30%

### When to Pause/Reduce
- Cost/Conversion > $50 for 7+ days
- No conversions in 5+ days
- CTR < 0.3%
- **Action**: Review targeting, pause underperforming assets

### Main vs India Comparison
After 2 weeks, compare:
- Which has better cost/conversion?
- Which has better conversion quality (do they become paying customers)?
- Reallocate budget to winner
